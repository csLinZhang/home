Payment by Scanning Palmprint

Introduction and Requirements

Modern mobile phones can obtain high-quality pamlprint images. Palmprint actually is very robust to kinds of variations and is more secure than face. However, its potentiality for commerce has not been fully investigated. Consider someday you will have an online payment system by scanning palmprint. It's cool!!

In this project, your task is to implement a palmprint verification system based on your mobile phone. Your project can be a cloud-client based architecture or can totally run on a mobile-phone.

Quite recently, our group has studied this problem and published a large-scale dataset. For more information, you can visit our project page at https://cslinzhang.github.io/MobilePalmPrint/
